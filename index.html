About Me

Five years bridging design, engineering and growth

I started as a self-taught designer obsessing over pixel grids, then learned to build the interfaces I was designing — and eventually the marketing systems that bring people to them. That full-stack view is now my edge: I don't hand off a Figma file and disappear, I design, build, ship and measure.

I've worked with early-stage founders validating their first product, agencies needing a senior hand on client work, and growth teams who needed a site that could actually rank and convert. Along the way I picked up a fourth skill set most designers don't have: training and evaluating AI systems, and writing the prompts that get them to behave.

Today I take on a small number of select projects at a time so each one gets the senior-level attention it deserves.

Case Study Walkthrough

Northbeam: from messy data to a dashboard people trust

A look at the full process behind the featured analytics dashboard redesign — from research to measured outcomes.

1

Research

8 user interviews and a heuristic audit surfaced 14 usability blockers in the existing dashboard.

2

Personas & Flows

Two primary personas and a simplified information architecture cut the reporting flow from 9 steps to 4.

3

Design System

A token-based system covering color, type, components and states — built for both light and dark contexts.

4

Prototype & Test

A clickable prototype tested with 6 users across two rounds, refining the chart and filter interactions.

5

Outcome

Shipped UI lifted task success by 34% and cut time-on-task by 41% in post-launch analytics.
